⚙️ Calculation method (estimates based on literature):
• Text: 0.001 Wh per word → (text pieces × words/piece) × 0.001 Wh
• Image: 1.0 Wh per image (range 0.5–2.5 Wh, average used)
• Audio (1 min): 3.0 Wh per clip (based on 0.5 Wh per 10 seconds × 6)
🌍 Conversion references:
• Washing machine: 500 Wh per full cycle → runtime (min) = (Energy_Wh / 500) × 60
• Rural African home baseline: 200 Wh/day → percentage = (Energy_Wh / 200) × 100
• LED bulb 10W: runtime (min) = (Energy_Wh / 10) × 60
• Smartphone charge: 15 Wh per full charge → charges = Energy_Wh / 15
• 💧 Water: ~0.00025 L per Wh (0.25 L per kWh) — based on Google 2023 & Ren et al. 2023. Older overestimates (0.5 L/Wh) were incorrect by a factor of 2000x.
• Monthly equivalent: daily energy × 30 days

📌 Disclaimer: All numbers are illustrative. Real-world AI energy depends on model size (GPT‑3 vs efficient model), hardware, data center PUE, and geographical factors. Water usage varies significantly with cooling technology and local climate.
